# Lumen Browser - English Version

Welcome to **Lumen**, your lightweight and customizable browser.

## Features

- Tabbed browsing.
- Search engines: Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Brave.
- Incognito tab (🕶) that does not save cookies, searches, or logins.
- Browser color customization (🎨).
- Cookie management (🍪) – easily delete cookies.
- Integrated search bar.

## How to use

1. Type the URL or search term in the address bar.
2. Click 🔍 to select your search engine.
3. Click 🕶 to open/close incognito tabs.
4. Click 🎨 to choose your browser color.
5. Click 🍪 and then “Delete Cookies” to clear all cookies.
